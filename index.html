PEOPLE AT ZERO

About Us

People At Zero is a three-piece band blending Blues Rock, Reggae, and Jazz into a powerful, groove-driven sound. Built on the raw foundation of guitar, bass, and drums, the trio delivers tight musicianship and undeniable chemistry. All music is original, with serious, thought-provoking lyrics written by guitarist Don — songs that make you dance, listen, and think. Though the lineup has evolved over time, the spirit and vision of the band remain unchanged — authentic, message-driven music rooted in rhythm, soul, and depth.
