PEOPLE AT ZERO

About Us

People At Zero is a three-piece band blending Blues Rock, Reggae, and Jazz into a powerful, groove-driven sound. Built on the raw foundation of guitar, bass, and drums, the trio delivers tight musicianship and undeniable chemistry. All music is original, with serious, thought-provoking lyrics written by guitarist Don — songs that make you dance, listen, and think.

Band Members

Don – Guitar & Vocals

Founder and main songwriter. Don crafts powerful lyrics and blues-driven riffs.

Jamie – Bass

The groove architect blending reggae bounce with jazz-influenced bass lines.

Honore – Drums

Driving the heartbeat with tight rock energy and subtle jazz textures.
